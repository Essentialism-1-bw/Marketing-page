What Is Essentialism?

Some people think Essentialism is just about getting more things done, but it’s actually about figuring out how you can get the right things done. It doesn’t mean doing less for the sake of less either. Itis about investing your energy and time in the best possible ways, so that you can operate at your highest point of contribution by doing only what is essential.

In a world with so many choices, it’s become harder than ever to decide what’s important. Think about it...

Nowadays everyone seems to overvalue the nonessentials, like a nicer car or house. Intangibles have taken the place of what’s truly essential in our lives. We place more value on the number of likes we receive on our Facebook posts, or how many followers we have on Twitter.

As a result, we sometimes neglect people or activities that are really essential, like spending time with loved ones, nurturing our gifts, even taking care of our health. We need to stop celebrating being busy as a measurement of importance.
‍

Essentialism is a disciplined, systematic approach for determining where your highest point of contribution lies. Once you master this, you will find that it makes the execution of those things almost effortless. What do you feel deeply inspired by? and What are you particularly talented at? and What meets a significant need in your world?

The Essentialist lives by design, not by default. Just Imagine what you could accomplish in your life If you could deliberately distinguish the vital few from the trivial many. If you eliminate the nonessentials and you remove those obstacles the essential things in your life would receive the time and attention needed to grow.

Where can you start? A great place to start is by signing up, we are here to help you on your journey.  You should take your time and decide what’s truly essential to you. Making a list of those things will help you to keep them in focus. Taking your first steps in the right direction is easier than you think. You can do this and we can help.
